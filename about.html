About Idea

Idea store sells the top brands of tech devices like laptops, tablets, smartphones and more so you get certified devices at affordable prices. We also pay cash-on-the-spot for your used devices and our Pit Crew offers repairs and upgrades quickly, for less.

When you stop by your local Idea, you’ll find constantly changing inventory so you can trade-up your laptop, tablet, cell phone or PC for a price that fits your budget. And, you can alos buy home appliences from different brands, there’s no waiting around or making return trips – you’ll get your choice of cash-on-the-spot or a trade-in credit.

We minimize the impact of technology on the environment by reusing devices and various parts where we can and recycling what can’t be repaired or reused.
